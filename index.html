METHODOLOGY

HOW IT WORKS

A four-stage pipeline from raw NBA API data to calibrated win probabilities.

01
📡
Data Fetch
10 seasons of playoff game logs pulled via nba_api. Rate-limited at 0.7s between requests across 2,800+ games.
02
⚙️
Feature Engineering
Home − Away differential stats computed for OffRtg, DefRtg, Pace, eFG%, 3P%, TOV rate, rolling plus-minus, and rest days.
03
🧠
Temporal Training
Logistic Regression with StandardScaler. Trained on seasons 2014–2022, tested strictly on 2022–2024 — no data leakage.
04
📊
Live Prediction
Model fetches current season team averages from the NBA API, computes differentials, and outputs calibrated win probabilities.
MODEL INPUTS

KEY FEATURES

22+ engineered differential stats power every prediction. Here are the heaviest hitters.

DIFF_OFF_RTG_PROXY
Offensive rating differential — points scored per 100 possessions, Home minus Away.
DIFF_DEF_RTG_PROXY
Defensive rating differential — points allowed per 100 possessions. Lower is better.
DIFF_EFG_PCT
Effective FG% differential — accounts for the extra value of three-pointers vs two-pointers.
DIFF_TOV_RATE
Turnover rate differential — turnovers per 100 possessions. High turnover teams lose playoff games.
DIFF_REST_DAYS
Rest day advantage. Teams with more rest show a measurable edge in playoff fatigue situations.
DIFF_ROLL_PLUS_MINUS
Rolling 5-game plus-minus differential — captures recent momentum heading into a game.
